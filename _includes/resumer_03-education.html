Shanghai American School (Puxi Campus) 2016-2023 | Middle School - High School (Grades 6-12)

I have been a student at Shanghai American School since 6th grade, and currently I am a senior. Some of the advanced courses I have taken as a high-schooler include AP United States History, AP Physics 1, AP Language and Composition, AP Psychology, and IB Chinese Mandarin B HL.

Involved in many school clubs such as Computer Science club, Robotics club, Make a Child Smile club and more. First joined the SAS high school JV baseball team in 9th grade, I also participated in the JV badminton team for a year in 10th grade.

Shanghai Fudan Wanke Experimental Private School (International Division) 2010-2015 | Elementary School (Grades 1-5)

Although this is a local Shanghai private school, I was participating under the international division where all classes except Math and Chinese were taught in English.

Some of my favorite activites include the yearly spelling bee and speech competitions, in which I have won multiple awards each year.

Due to a sudden illness that brought me on a world-wide journey for a diagnosis, I did not attend the first semester of 5th grade here.

E.H. Greene Intermediate School 2015 | Elementary School (First Semester of 5th Grade)

While I was receiving treatment in Cincinnati for my illness CRMO (Chronic recurrent multifocal osteomyelitis) I attended the local public elementary school to not drop my academics.

I was a new student and one of the few Asians, while also being on crutches due to having a hole in my leg from a bone marrow biopsy. Despite short lasting, I made some great friends here that helped make my experience much more pleasant. Here I also got to interact with a much more diverse student body, leanring more about what life is like outside of China.

The following semester I returned back to the Fudan Wanke School in Shanghai as I was almost fully recovered.
